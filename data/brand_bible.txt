CLIENT: FITFLOW
Tagline: "Move More. Live Better."
================================================================================

1. BRAND OVERVIEW
-----------------
FitFlow is a premium digital fitness platform targeting women aged 25–45 who want
sustainable, science-backed fitness — not fad diets or extreme regimens.
We exist at the intersection of empathy and performance. Our users are busy, smart,
and skeptical of empty promises. We earn their trust by keeping ours.

2. BRAND VOICE & TONE
---------------------
ENERGETIC but not aggressive. EMPOWERING but not preachy. HONEST but not blunt.
We speak like a knowledgeable friend — warm, direct, and real.

DO use: 'you', 'your', conversational contractions, active voice.
DO NOT use: 'lose weight fast', 'burn fat', 'shred', 'no pain no gain', 'guilt',
'lazy', 'cheat day', exclamation marks (max 1 per ad unit), all-caps for emphasis.

3. APPROVED MESSAGING PILLARS
------------------------------
Pillar 1 — SUSTAINABILITY: long-term habits over quick fixes.
Pillar 2 — PERSONALISATION: adaptive coaching engine, 'fits your life', 'your pace'.
Pillar 3 — COMMUNITY: reference 200,000+ member community when relevant.
Pillar 4 — SCIENCE-BACKED: 'expert trainers', 'science-backed', 'clinically informed'.

4. CLAIMS POLICY
----------------
PROHIBITED:
- Specific weight loss guarantees (e.g. 'lose 10 lbs in 2 weeks')
- Medical or therapeutic claims ('cures', 'treats', 'heals')
- Comparative superlatives without evidence ('#1 fitness app in the world')
- Before/after body transformation framing implying guaranteed results
- Body image language: 'fat', 'skinny', 'slim down', 'get thin', 'tone up' (as weight-loss synonym)
- Competitor references — never name a competitor, no implied comparisons
- Urgency manipulation: 'last chance', 'only X left', 'don't miss out' (unless factually verified)
- Hyperbole: 'best ever', 'world-class' (unless licensed), 'revolutionary', 'groundbreaking'

APPROVED:
- 'Up to X% of members report...' (with source)
- Aggregated social proof: '200,000 members and counting'
- 'Our trainers are certified by...'

5. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC RULES
---------------------------
META (Facebook/Instagram):
- Headlines: max 40 characters for best delivery
- Body: lead with the emotional hook in the first 125 characters
- CTA buttons: 'Shop Now', 'Learn More', 'Sign Up' preferred; custom CTAs in body copy allowed
- No direct price comparisons with named competitors

GOOGLE (Search):
- Headlines: max 30 characters, no punctuation at the end
- Headline 1 must include the brand name or primary keyword
- Descriptions must include at least one call to action

TIKTOK:
- Hook must land in the first 3 seconds — mirror this in copy
- Casual, first-person or second-person framing preferred
- Avoid corporate language entirely
- Approved CTAs: 'Get Yours', 'Shop Now', 'Try It'

DISPLAY:
- Headline: max 25 characters — ultra-concise, benefit-led
- Body: max 1 short sentence (90 characters)
- Must include offer or proof point prominently

LINKEDIN:
- APPROVED for FitFlow B2B Corporate Wellness ONLY (targeting HR directors, People Ops, employee wellness managers)
- NOT approved for consumer direct-to-consumer FitFlow campaigns
- Tone must be professional and ROI-focused, not emotional/lifestyle
- Approved CTAs: 'Book a Demo', 'Get a Pilot', 'Learn More'

6. OFFER & PRICING GUIDELINES
------------------------------
Current approved offers (Q2 2025):
- 50% off premium annual plan (Summer Sale — approved through August 31, 2025)
- Free 7-day trial (evergreen — always approved)
- $1 first-month trial (Meta and Google only)
B2B (LinkedIn): 90-day pilot program available for teams of 50+

7. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE
----------------------
All copy subject to FTC endorsement guidelines. Testimonials must reflect typical
results unless marked 'individual results may vary'. Any ad referencing a specific
person requires a signed model release on file with Gupta Media legal.

CLIENT: ACME CORP
Product: AI-powered CRM for B2B sales teams
Tagline: "Close More. Work Less."
================================================================================

1. BRAND OVERVIEW
-----------------
Acme Corp sells an AI-powered CRM platform targeting B2B sales teams of 10–500
at growth-stage companies. Buyers are VP Sales, RevOps, and Sales Directors.
They are data-driven skeptics who have been burned by over-promised software before.
Every claim must feel earned and backed by specificity.

2. BRAND VOICE & TONE
---------------------
CONFIDENT but not arrogant. DATA-DRIVEN but not robotic. DIRECT but not aggressive.
Speak to the pain of inefficiency. Never use casual slang or consumer language.

DO use: specific outcomes ('40% more time selling'), active verbs, second-person ('your team').
DO NOT use: 'easy', 'simple', 'just' (minimizes real complexity), vague superlatives,
consumer-style urgency ('limited time!'), pricing without qualification.

3. APPROVED MESSAGING PILLARS
------------------------------
Pillar 1 — EFFICIENCY: less admin, more selling time. 'Eliminate busywork.'
Pillar 2 — INTELLIGENCE: AI-powered lead scoring, automated follow-ups.
Pillar 3 — SPEED: reach leads faster, pipeline velocity, response time.
Pillar 4 — PROOF: case studies, percentages, trial offers — always specific.

4. CLAIMS POLICY
----------------
PROHIBITED:
- Unqualified ROI claims ('double your revenue') without 'up to' qualifier
- Specific revenue guarantees
- Naming or implying competitor inferiority
- 'Easy' or 'simple' — these undermine enterprise credibility
- Pricing claims without 'starting at' or pre-approval from media team

APPROVED:
- 'Up to X% more time selling' (with 'based on customer data' qualifier)
- 'Teams using Acme close X% more deals' (with source)
- Free trial CTAs — always qualified with trial length

5. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC RULES
---------------------------
LINKEDIN:
- Primary channel. Professional tone mandatory.
- Headlines: problem-led or outcome-led. Max 70 characters.
- Approved CTAs: 'Book a Demo', 'See It in Action', 'Start Free Trial', 'Get a Quote'

GOOGLE (Search):
- Keyword-aligned headlines. Brand name in Headline 1.
- No punctuation at end of headlines. Max 30 characters per headline.
- CTAs must be in description, not headline.

META (Facebook):
- APPROVED for retargeting and lookalike audiences ONLY
- Tone should be warmer than LinkedIn but still professional
- No consumer-style emotional appeals

DISPLAY:
- Short, benefit-led. Approved for retargeting.
- Headline max 25 characters. Single clear CTA.

TIKTOK:
- NOT an approved channel for Acme Corp. Reject any TikTok copy for this client.

6. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE
----------------------
All performance statistics require 'based on customer data' or 'in internal study'.
No guaranteed outcome claims. Case studies require legal sign-off before referencing.

CLIENT: NOVASKIN
Product: Premium dermatologist-formulated skincare serum
Tagline: "Science You Can See."
================================================================================

1. BRAND OVERVIEW
-----------------
NovaSkin sells a premium anti-aging and renewal serum targeting women aged 35–60
who are science-literate, ingredient-conscious, and skeptical of over-hyped beauty claims.
They want proof, not promises. NovaSkin's differentiator is clinical credibility.

2. BRAND VOICE & TONE
---------------------
PREMIUM but accessible. SCIENCE-FORWARD but not cold. EMPOWERING but never fear-based.
We celebrate skin health without shaming natural aging. No before/after body framing.

DO use: 'clinically tested', 'dermatologist-formulated', 'visible results', 'science-backed'.
DO NOT use: 'miracle', 'fix', 'cure', 'anti-aging' without scientific qualifier,
fear-based language about aging, body-shaming or comparison language.

3. APPROVED MESSAGING PILLARS
------------------------------
Pillar 1 — CLINICAL CREDIBILITY: dermatologist-formulated, clinically tested.
Pillar 2 — VISIBLE RESULTS: 'visible results in X days' (requires 'in clinical study' qualifier).
Pillar 3 — COMMUNITY TRUST: '50,000+ customers', real reviews, community-approved.
Pillar 4 — PREMIUM QUALITY: ingredient quality, luxury positioning, 'your skin deserves'.

4. CLAIMS POLICY
----------------
PROHIBITED:
- 'Miracle', 'cure', 'fix', 'reverses aging' — too absolute, FTC risk
- Specific measurement claims ('reduces wrinkles by 70%') without 'in clinical study'
- Before/after transformation framing implying guaranteed results
- Age-shaming: never reference aging as a problem to be fixed
- Competitor comparisons by name
- 'Proven' without an evidence qualifier

APPROVED:
- 'Clinically tested' — always approved
- 'Visible results in 14 days' — approved with 'in clinical study, results may vary'
- 'Dermatologist-formulated' — always approved
- 'Up to X% improvement in [metric]' with 'in clinical study' qualifier

5. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC RULES
---------------------------
META (Facebook/Instagram):
- Visual-first mindset in copy — describe the sensory/lifestyle experience
- Headlines: max 40 characters
- Emotional + credibility combination works best
- Approved CTAs: 'Shop Now', 'Learn More'

GOOGLE (Search):
- Lead with clinical credibility in headline
- No punctuation at end of headlines. Max 30 characters.
- Include 'free shipping' or 'risk-free' offer in description when active

TIKTOK:
- Community and authenticity-first. 'Real results — no filters needed' framing approved.
- Hook in first line. Casual but credible.
- Approved CTAs: 'Shop Now', 'Get Yours'

DISPLAY:
- Ultra-concise. Headline max 25 characters. Lead with proof or offer.
- Single CTA only.

LINKEDIN:
- NOT an approved channel for NovaSkin. Consumer beauty brands are off-target for LinkedIn.

6. OFFER & PRICING GUIDELINES
------------------------------
Current approved offers (Q2 2025):
- Free shipping on first order (evergreen)
- 30-day money-back guarantee (evergreen — always approved to reference)
- 15% off first order with email sign-up (Meta and Display only)

7. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE
----------------------
All dermatological claims must include 'Results may vary' in ad disclaimers.
'Clinically tested' requires the full disclaimer: 'In a clinical study of X participants'.
No claims that imply treatment of medical skin conditions (eczema, psoriasis, acne as disease).
